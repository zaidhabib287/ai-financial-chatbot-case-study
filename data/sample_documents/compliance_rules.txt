FINANCIAL COMPLIANCE RULES
Version 2.0 - Effective January 1, 2024

TRANSACTION LIMITS:

1. Individual Customer Limits:
   - Daily transfer limit: 1000 BHD (Bahraini Dinars)
   - Per transaction limit: 500 BHD
   - Monthly transfer limit: 20000 BHD

2. Corporate Customer Limits:
   - Daily transfer limit: 50000 BHD
   - Per transaction limit: 10000 BHD
   - Monthly transfer limit: 500000 BHD

3. Enhanced Due Diligence Requirements:
   - Transactions over 100 BHD require identity verification
   - Transactions over 500 BHD require source of funds documentation
   - Transactions over 1000 BHD require management approval

4. Restricted Activities:
   - Cash transactions over 5000 BHD are prohibited
   - Third-party transfers require written authorization
   - International transfers require sanctions screening

5. Reporting Requirements:
   - All suspicious transactions must be reported within 24 hours
   - Daily transaction reports must be submitted to compliance
   - Monthly compliance summaries required for audit
